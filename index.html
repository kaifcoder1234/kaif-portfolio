About Bhatpura Rasoolpur

Bhatpura Rasoolpur is a beautiful village located in Powayan Tehsil, Shahjahanpur district, Uttar Pradesh. The village has schools, mosques, sports grounds and hardworking people. This website is dedicated to sharing information about the village, its culture, education and sports.
